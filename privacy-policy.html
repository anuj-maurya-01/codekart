Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

CodeKart respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Data: name, email address, login details.
  • Order Data: products purchased, billing details, transaction IDs.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage logs.
  • Support Data: messages and attachments shared with our support team.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and improve CodeKart services, including:

  • Creating and managing your account.
  • Processing orders, downloads, and refunds.
  • Sending transactional updates and support responses.
  • Improving platform performance, security, and product quality.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on one or more of these legal grounds: performance of a contract, legal obligations, legitimate business interests, and your consent where required.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers such as payment gateways, analytics providers, and hosting partners who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, maintain sessions, analyze usage, and improve user experience. You can control cookies through browser settings.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution obligations.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your data, and to request data portability.

10. Children's Privacy

CodeKart is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent where required.

11. Third-Party Links

Our site may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

13. Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@codekart.com.
